**Code of Conduct**

Our Pledge
I, as the sole maintainer of KernelCTC, pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone who contributes, opens issues, or engages in any way.
Standards
I encourage:

Clear, respectful communication in issues and PRs
Constructive feedback and suggestions
Patience — this is a solo project maintained in spare time

I will not tolerate:

Harassment or disrespectful language of any kind
Spam or low-effort contributions
Pressure to prioritize features or fixes on demand

Enforcement
This project is maintained by one person. If something feels off, reach out at **ABHIVAADYA.SHARMA@gmail.com** and I'll handle it as fairly and promptly as I can.
